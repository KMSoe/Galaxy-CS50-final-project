WHAT IS Galaxy

Galaxy is e-commerce platform for shopping, founded by Kaung Myat Soe and May Thu Aung in 2018. As started, the goal was to make easier for customers to shop various products sold by individuals and small business within Mandalay

With the the trust of customers, the company has improved and severals suppliers and small business have joined our community.

Now, our Galaxy Platform can be used around the country. We serve with many staffs to help the customers' daily lives.

Our Mission

To support our customers and suppliers with a platform offering easier ways to shop, delivery services, time and energy save and so forth.

Our Mission is To satisfy our customers, to be creative in their valuable time, with our easy, quick, and trustful shopping services.

What We Do?

We create the platform for the customers and suppliers. There are many products on our Galaxy platform supported by individuals and small business around the country. Customers can buy these products through our platform and then, we deliver them to the buyers.

In these three cities: Mandalay, Yangon, Naypyitaw, there is Home Delivery Service and cash on delivery.

In other cities, customers need one of prepaid types and orders will be delivered by Postal system within a few hours after the bill had been received.
